What is Assassin Live?

Welcome to Assassin Live! Assassin is a social, live-action game in which each player has a target to assassinate. Once a player assassinates their target, they will gain the target of the player they assassinated. This process continues until only one player remains, who will then be crowned the champion.

Assassin Live is an application to organize the play of this game. We realized that, especially with large games, it can be hard for a moderator to keep up with everything that is going on. For moderators, we offer lots of freedom and flexibility to fit most rulesets. However, if you don't have any special rules, the default ruleset works very well.

Here is the Wikipedia page for Assassin.

How can I play?

To play, {% if not user %} after you log in,{% endif %} you can either start a game and get your friends to join or join an existing game.

If you start a game, you will be moderating the game for your friends, where you will be able to view all of the player’s targets and moderate disputes. Moderating can be just as fun as playing!

If you join a game, you will be entered as a regular player.
